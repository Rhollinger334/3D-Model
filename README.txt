Widowmaker (Overwatch) 3D Model Archive
========================================

Contents
--------
data/
  widowmaker_ULTIMATE_measurements.json  — 120+ precise facial/body measurements
  widowmaker_ALL_models.json             — Full vertex/mesh data for all models

models/OW1/
  *_Textured_Default.glb   — OW1 default skin (diffuse textures embedded)
  *_Textured_Noire.glb     — OW1 Noire skin  (diffuse textures embedded)
  *_Full_Body.glb          — OW1 full body + weapon (no textures)
  *_Body_Only.glb          — OW1 body only  (no textures)
  *_Head_Only.glb          — OW1 head only  (no textures)

models/OW2_Talon/
  *_Talon_Full.glb         — OW2 Talon full body (single texture atlas)
  *_Talon_Head.glb         — OW2 Talon head only (single texture atlas)

models/OW2/
  *_Head_No_Helmet.glb     — OW2 base head, no helmet/hair (wireframe)

Skin tone (Talon model, sampled from texture):
  Base:     RGB(215, 220, 225)  #D7DCE1
  Median:   RGB(202, 201, 205)  #CAC9CD
  Shadow:   RGB(170, 168, 170)  #AAA8AA
  Highlight:RGB(219, 222, 225)  #DBDEE1

Note: Full OW2 head variants (with/without visor, 37-46 MB each) are served
by the web app but excluded from this archive due to size.
